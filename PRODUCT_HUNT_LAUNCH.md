## Launch Strategy

### Timing

**Best Days to Launch**:
- **Tuesday, Wednesday, or Thursday** (most active days on Product Hunt)
- **Avoid**: Monday (busy catching up), Friday (weekend distraction), weekends

**Best Time**:
- **12:01 AM PST** (midnight Pacific Time)
- This gives you the full 24 hours to gain momentum
- Product Hunt day starts at midnight PST

**Recommended Launch Date**:
- **Tuesday, January 14, 2026** or **Thursday, January 16, 2026**

## Common Product Hunt Questions (Prepare Answers)

### Q: "How does it make money if it's free?"
**A:** It doesn't! This is a passion project to prove privacy-first health tracking is possible. No ads, no premium tiers. I might accept donations in the future, but the core will always be free.

### Q: "Why not use Firebase or Supabase for sync?"
**A:** Privacy. Any backend introduces a server that can be hacked, subpoenaed, or sold. Local-only ensures YOUR data stays YOURS. Optional self-hosted sync is on the roadmap.

### Q: "What about HIPAA compliance?"
**A:** StepSyncAI isn't a HIPAA-covered entity (it's a personal tool, not a healthcare provider). Your data never touches a server, so HIPAA doesn't apply. If you share exports with your doctor, their system must be HIPAA-compliant.

### Q: "Can I import data from Apple Health / Google Fit?"
**A:** Not yet, but wearable integration is planned. For now, you can manually log data. Future versions will support API integration.

### Q: "Why vanilla JavaScript instead of React?"
**A:** Zero dependencies (except Chart.js), instant loading, simple deployment, easy auditing. The codebase is small enough that vanilla JS is actually simpler than managing a framework.

### Q: "Is there an iOS/Android app?"
**A:** It's a PWA (Progressive Web App), so you can install it like a native app on both platforms. Native app wrappers (React Native) are on the roadmap.
